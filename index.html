Qui suis-je ?

Texte de description.

Hello and thanks for checking my work !

I’m Clement, I’m a game audio designer for video game and I’m currently searching for an end-of-studies internship in the industry. My love for audio and video games goes back when I was really young and since then I never ceased to be immersed in anything related to these 2 wonderful worlds, eager to bring my knowledges and vision on incredible games. I have always been able to acquired new self-taught skills and applied them on professional contexts especially during my studies at CNAM ENJMIN Master’s degree in Audio Design in video games.
